About Harrison Pest Management

Your local pest control expert.

I bring professional-grade expertise directly to your property. I began my career in mosquito abatement in 2004, quickly rising to Acting Director at Canyon County Mosquito Abatement District, where I implemented more effective strategies for equipment use and field operations.

Most recently, I served as Mosquito Abatement Supervisor for Bannock County, overseeing a full team of technicians performing larviciding, fogging, and mosquito trapping and testing across the entire county.

Between my abatement roles, I spent nearly 17 years as a Senior Service Specialist with Ecolab, one of the world's leading pest and hygiene solution companies. During that time, I consistently maintained a 92–99% customer retention rate across a caseload of up to 120 accounts — a track record that speaks for itself.

When you hire me, you're not getting a technician who just read the manual. You're getting someone who wrote the operational manual — literally — and has the field experience to back it up.
